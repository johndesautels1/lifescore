## 1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit websites. They help websites remember your preferences, understand how you use the site, and improve your experience.

Similar technologies include:
- **Local Storage:** Data stored in your browser
- **Session Storage:** Temporary data cleared when you close the browser
- **Pixels/Beacons:** Tiny images that track page views

This policy covers all these technologies (collectively "cookies").

## 5. Managing Cookies

### 5.1 Cookie Consent Banner

When you first visit our site, you will see a cookie consent banner. You can:

- **Accept All:** Enable all cookie categories
- **Reject Non-Essential:** Only essential cookies will be used
- **Customize:** Choose which categories to allow

### 5.2 Changing Your Preferences

You can change your cookie preferences at any time:

1. Click the "Cookie Settings" link in the website footer
2. Or go to Account Settings > Privacy > Cookie Preferences

### 5.3 Browser Settings

You can also control cookies through your browser:

| Browser | Instructions |
|---------|--------------|
| Chrome | Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies |
| Firefox | Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies |
| Safari | Preferences > Privacy > Cookies |
| Edge | Settings > Privacy > Cookies |

**Note:** Blocking essential cookies may prevent the Service from working correctly.

### 5.4 Do Not Track

Some browsers send "Do Not Track" signals. We currently do not respond to DNT signals but honor your cookie consent choices.

## 6. Local Storage and Session Storage

We use browser storage for:

| Key | Purpose | Type |
|-----|---------|------|
| `savedComparisons` | Cache your saved comparisons | Local Storage |
| `recentCities` | Remember recently compared cities | Local Storage |

This data stays on your device and is not sent to our servers unless you're logged in and syncing.

To clear: Browser Settings > Clear Browsing Data > Cookies and Site Data
