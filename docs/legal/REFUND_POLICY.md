# Refund Policy

**Clues Intelligence LTD**
**Effective Date:** January 23, 2026
**Last Updated:** January 23, 2026

---

## 1. Overview

This Refund Policy explains when and how you can receive refunds for CLUES Comparison Reports services, including LIFE SCORE subscriptions and report purchases.

---

## 2. Subscription Refunds

### 2.1 Monthly Subscriptions

| Situation | Refund |
|-----------|--------|
| Cancel anytime | No refund for current month; access continues until period ends |
| Service unavailable >24 hours | Pro-rated credit for downtime |
| Billing error | Full refund of erroneous charges |

### 2.2 Annual Subscriptions

| Situation | Refund |
|-----------|--------|
| Cancel within 14 days | Full refund minus any reports generated |
| Cancel after 14 days | No refund; access continues until period ends |
| Service unavailable >48 hours | Pro-rated credit |
| Billing error | Full refund of erroneous charges |

### 2.3 Enterprise Plans

Enterprise refunds are governed by your individual contract. Contact your account manager.

---

## 3. Report Purchase Refunds

### 3.1 Generated Reports

**No refunds after report generation.**

Once a comparison report is generated:
- AI processing costs are incurred
- The report is immediately available
- The data cannot be "un-generated"

### 3.2 Failed Report Generation

If a report fails to generate due to our error:
- Automatic credit for another generation
- Or full refund if preferred

### 3.3 Report Quality Issues

If you believe a report contains significant errors:
1. Contact support within 7 days
2. Explain the specific issues
3. We will investigate and may offer:
   - Corrected report at no charge
   - Credit for future report
   - Refund in exceptional cases

**Note:** AI-generated content may vary. Minor differences in scoring are not grounds for refund.

---

## 4. How to Request a Refund

### 4.1 Self-Service
1. Go to Account Settings > Billing
2. Click "Request Refund"
3. Select reason and submit

### 4.2 Email Request
Email: billing@cluesintelligence.com

Include:
- Account email
- Transaction date and amount
- Reason for refund request

### 4.3 Processing Time

| Method | Response Time | Refund Processing |
|--------|---------------|-------------------|
| Self-service | Immediate (if eligible) | 5-10 business days |
| Email | Within 2 business days | 5-10 business days |

Refunds are returned to the original payment method.

---

## 5. Non-Refundable Items

The following are NOT eligible for refunds:

- Reports already generated and delivered
- Subscriptions cancelled after the refund window
- Accounts terminated for policy violations
- Promotional or discounted purchases (unless stated)
- Third-party fees (payment processor charges)

---

## 6. Chargebacks

If you dispute a charge with your bank instead of contacting us:
- We will provide transaction evidence to the bank
- Your account may be suspended pending resolution
- Fraudulent chargebacks may result in account termination

**Please contact us first** - we're happy to resolve issues directly.

---

## 7. Credits vs. Refunds

In some cases, we may offer account credit instead of a cash refund:

| Credit Amount | Validity |
|---------------|----------|
| Up to £20 | 6 months |
| £20 - £100 | 12 months |
| Over £100 | 24 months |

Credits can be used for:
- Subscription payments
- Report purchases
- Feature upgrades

Credits are non-transferable and have no cash value.

---

## 8. Currency and Taxes

- Refunds are issued in the original payment currency
- Taxes paid are refunded proportionally
- Exchange rate fluctuations are not compensated

---

## 9. Special Circumstances

### 9.1 Death or Serious Illness
We handle these cases with compassion. Contact us with documentation.

### 9.2 Natural Disasters / Force Majeure
Extended refund windows may apply in affected regions.

### 9.3 Service Discontinuation
If we discontinue the Service:
- Active subscribers receive pro-rated refunds
- 30 days notice provided where possible

---

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes apply to purchases made after the update date. Material changes will be communicated via email.
