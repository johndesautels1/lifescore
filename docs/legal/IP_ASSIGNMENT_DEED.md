### 2. SCOPE OF RIGHTS

2.1 The assignment in Section 1 includes all rights of every kind and nature, throughout the world, in perpetuity, including but not limited to:

(a) All copyrights, database rights, and rights in software;
(b) All rights to apply for registered trademarks, patents, or other registered intellectual property rights;
(c) All trade secrets, know-how, and confidential information;
(d) All moral rights (to the extent permitted by law — see Section 3);
(e) All rights to bring actions for past infringement;
(f) All rights of action in respect of any infringement of any of the above rights, whether occurring before, on, or after the date of this Deed.

### 3. MORAL RIGHTS

3.1 To the extent permitted under the Copyright, Designs and Patents Act 1988 and any other applicable law, the Assignor hereby unconditionally and irrevocably waives all moral rights in the Assigned IP in favour of the Assignee, its successors, licensees, and assigns.

3.2 The Assignor shall not assert any moral rights (including the right to be identified as author and the right to object to derogatory treatment) in respect of the Assigned IP against the Assignee or any party authorised by the Assignee.

### 4. WARRANTIES

The Assignor warrants and represents to the Assignee that:

4.1 The Assignor is the sole owner of the Assigned IP and has full right, power, and authority to enter into this Deed and to assign the Assigned IP.

4.2 The Assigned IP is original work created by the Assignor (with the assistance of AI tools as stated in Recital D) and does not infringe the intellectual property rights of any third party.

4.3 No third-party freelancers, contractors, or external developers contributed to the Assigned IP. No other person has any claim, right, title, or interest in or to the Assigned IP.

4.4 The Assigned IP is free from all encumbrances, liens, charges, licences (other than end-user licences granted in the ordinary course of business), and adverse claims.

4.5 There are no outstanding disputes, claims, or proceedings relating to the Assigned IP, and the Assignor is not aware of any circumstances that might give rise to any such dispute, claim, or proceeding.

4.6 Open-source components used in the software are used under their respective licences and do not affect the ownership of the proprietary Assigned IP.

### 5. FURTHER ASSURANCE

5.1 The Assignor shall, at the expense of the Assignee, execute such further documents and do such further acts and things as the Assignee may reasonably require from time to time to give full effect to this Deed, including:

(a) Signing any applications for registration of trademarks, patents, or other intellectual property rights;
(b) Signing any documents required to record the assignment with any intellectual property registry;
(c) Providing any declarations, evidence, or assistance required in connection with any intellectual property proceedings.

### 6. CONSIDERATION

6.1 This assignment is made for good and valuable consideration, the receipt and sufficiency of which is hereby acknowledged by the Assignor, consisting of:

(a) The Assignor's position as sole director and sole shareholder of the Assignee;

### 9. ENTIRE AGREEMENT

9.1 This Deed constitutes the entire agreement between the parties with respect to the assignment of the Assigned IP and supersedes all prior agreements, representations, and understandings relating thereto.
