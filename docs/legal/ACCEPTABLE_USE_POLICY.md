# Acceptable Use Policy

**Clues Intelligence LTD**
**Effective Date:** January 23, 2026
**Last Updated:** January 23, 2026

---

## 1. Introduction

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") governs your use of CLUES Comparison Reports services, including LIFE SCORE and future comparison products (collectively, the "Service"). By using the Service, you agree to comply with this policy.

---

## 2. Permitted Uses

You MAY use the Service to:

- Compare cities for personal relocation decisions
- Research locations for business expansion
- Generate reports for internal business use
- Share reports with family, colleagues, or professional advisors
- Quote or reference reports with attribution to "CLUES Intelligence"
- Make informed decisions about where to live, work, or invest
- Use AI assistant (Olivia) for questions about comparison data

---

## 3. Prohibited Uses

You may NOT use the Service to:

### 3.1 Illegal Activities
- Violate any applicable laws or regulations
- Facilitate illegal immigration or visa fraud
- Evade law enforcement or regulatory authorities
- Plan or execute illegal activities

### 3.2 Commercial Misuse
- Resell, redistribute, or white-label reports for profit
- Create derivative products or services for sale
- Use data to build competing services
- Scrape, harvest, or bulk-download content
- Use automated tools (bots, scrapers) to access the Service

### 3.3 System Abuse
- Attempt to reverse-engineer our AI systems or algorithms
- Circumvent rate limits or access controls
- Exploit vulnerabilities or security weaknesses
- Overwhelm the Service with excessive requests
- Interfere with other users' access to the Service

### 3.4 Misrepresentation
- Present AI-generated content as official government data
- Claim reports constitute legal, financial, or professional advice
- Impersonate other users or Clues staff
- Submit false information or fraudulent payment details
- Create multiple accounts to circumvent restrictions

### 3.5 Harmful Content
- Submit content that is defamatory, obscene, or threatening
- Harass, abuse, or harm other users
- Transmit malware, viruses, or malicious code
- Use the Service to send spam or unsolicited communications

### 3.6 AI Assistant Misuse
- Attempt to manipulate Olivia into providing harmful information
- Use AI conversations to generate illegal content
- Share personally identifiable information of others
- Attempt "jailbreak" or prompt injection attacks

| Severity | Examples | Action |
|----------|----------|--------|

1. Email legal@cluesintelligence.com within 14 days

Accounts terminated for policy violations are not eligible for refunds.
